Who are the FrogRats?

We are a group of nerds that met during our first year studying Computer Science at Nottingham Trent University (NTU).

After working on a few projects together, we decided to form our little GitHub organisation as a way to compile and showcase all of our different projects. You can find out more about our projects via our GitHub.
What about the FrogRat mascot you ask? We just thought it looked cute and decided to stick with the name.
